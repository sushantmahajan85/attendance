EXCHANGE FOR A CHANGE

Thapar Food Festival has taken an initiative to make 'Plastic Waste Problem' a thing of the past. Starting on 13th August, under this campaign, people will be taking up plegde to recycle all their plastic waste instead of throwing it away. As a push, all those who want to join us in the campaign are invited to become a part of it by bringing an empty recyclable plastic bottle to us at COS Complex starting from 13th August, and getting free food deal coupons in exchange.
